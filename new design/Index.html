A Hybrid Designer + Developer.

I am a Melbourne based Graphic Designer and Web Designer/Developer. Over the years I have strived to a find balance between functionality and aesthetics. I find that I am a quick and eager learner and very much inspired by new developments, theories and technology.

My key is to always look on the bright side of life. Indulge in food and coffee, and to be taken away with music, design and art.

I completed a Bachelor of Design Arts (Graphic Design) at the Australian Academy of Design in Port Melbourne (2008). And mastered skills over the years in the following programs and languages: Adobe CS Suite including InDesign, Illustrator, Photoshop, Edge Code, Flash and Dreamweaver. HTML, CCS, Javascript, Terminal, Github, SourceTree and Sublime Text 2.
